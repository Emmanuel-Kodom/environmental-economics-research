# Environmental Economics Research

This repository contains empirical research projects in **environmental and development economics**, implemented in **R** using reproducible workflows.

The projects focus on the relationship between economic development, institutions, and environmental outcomes, with applications to both the United States and African countries.

---

## Projects

### 1. Environmental Kuznets Curve in the United States
Empirical analysis testing the Environmental Kuznets Curve (EKC) hypothesis using U.S. data.

### 2. Education, Institutions, and Environmental Quality in Africa
Panel data analysis examining how human capital development and institutional quality affect pollution and sustainability outcomes in African countries.

---

## Methods
- Panel data econometrics
- Fixed effects models
- Robust and clustered standard errors
