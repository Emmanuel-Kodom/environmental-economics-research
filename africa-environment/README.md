# Education, Institutions, and Environmental Quality in Africa

This project investigates how **human capital development and institutional quality** affect environmental pollution and sustainability outcomes across African countries.

---

## Research Question
How do education and institutions influence environmental outcomes in Africa?

---

## Data
Panel data on African countries.

---

## Methods
- Fixed effects panel regressions
- Robust and clustered standard errors

---

## Status
Ongoing working paper.
