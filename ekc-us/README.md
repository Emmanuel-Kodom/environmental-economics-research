# Environmental Kuznets Curve in the United States

This project empirically tests the **Environmental Kuznets Curve (EKC)** hypothesis, which posits an inverted-U relationship between economic development and environmental pollution.

---

## Research Question
Does economic growth initially increase pollution and later reduce it as income rises?

---

## Data
U.S. macroeconomic and environmental indicators.

---

## Methods
- Panel data regression
- Fixed effects
- Quadratic income specification

---

## Status
Coursework-based empirical project.
